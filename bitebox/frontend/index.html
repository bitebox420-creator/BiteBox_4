Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about implementing BiteBox

How does BiteBox integrate with our existing systems?

BiteBox integrates seamlessly with existing POS systems and student ID cards. Our team handles the technical integration, including barcode/RFID readers and existing payment gateways. Most schools are fully operational within 2-3 weeks.

What hardware is required for deployment?

BiteBox runs on standard tablets or POS terminals. We provide optional hardware packages including order kiosks, kitchen displays, and RFID readers. The system works with existing devices in most cases.

How is student data secured?

We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for all data. Student health and payment information is stored securely with strict access controls. We comply with data protection regulations and never share personal data with third parties.

What does BiteBox cost?

Our pricing is based on school size and features needed. We offer flexible plans starting from a small per-student fee. Schedule a demo to get a customized quote tailored to your school's requirements.
